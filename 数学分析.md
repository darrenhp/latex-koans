# 数学分析核心概念速记
> 极简文字，纯LaTeX公式核心定义

## 1. 实数与极限
### 数列极限定义
$$
\forall \varepsilon>0, \exists N \in \mathbb{N}^+, \forall n>N: |a_n - A| < \varepsilon \implies \lim_{n \to \infty} a_n = A
$$

### 函数极限定义
$$
\forall \varepsilon>0, \exists \delta>0, \forall x: 0<|x-x_0|<\delta: |f(x)-A|<\varepsilon \implies \lim_{x \to x_0} f(x) = A
$$

### 无穷小量
$$
\lim_{x \to x_0} \alpha(x) = 0
$$

## 2. 连续性
### 连续定义
$$
\lim_{x \to x_0} f(x) = f(x_0)
$$

### 一致连续
$$
\forall \varepsilon>0, \exists \delta>0, \forall x_1,x_2 \in I: |x_1-x_2|<\delta \implies |f(x_1)-f(x_2)|<\varepsilon
$$

## 3. 微分学
### 导数定义
$$
f'(x_0) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x_0+\Delta x)-f(x_0)}{\Delta x} = \frac{dy}{dx}
$$

### 微分
$$
dy = f'(x)dx
$$

### 中值定理
**拉格朗日中值定理**
$$
f(b)-f(a) = f'(\xi)(b-a),\ \xi \in (a,b)
$$

**柯西中值定理**
$$
\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{g(b)-g(a)} = \frac{f'(\xi)}{g'(\xi)},\ \xi \in (a,b)
$$

### 泰勒公式
$$
f(x) = \sum_{k=0}^n \frac{f^{(k)}(x_0)}{k!}(x-x_0)^k + o((x-x_0)^n)
$$

## 4. 积分学
### 黎曼积分定义
$$
\int_a^b f(x)dx = \lim_{\lambda \to 0} \sum_{i=1}^n f(\xi_i)\Delta x_i
$$

### 牛顿-莱布尼茨公式
$$
\int_a^b f(x)dx = F(b) - F(a),\ F'(x)=f(x)
$$

### 反常积分
$$
\int_a^{+\infty} f(x)dx = \lim_{A \to +\infty} \int_a^A f(x)dx
$$

### 常用积分公式
**基本积分表**
$$
\begin{aligned}
&\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \quad (n \neq -1),\quad
\int \frac{1}{x}dx = \ln|x| + C \\
&\int e^x dx = e^x + C,\quad
\int a^x dx = \frac{a^x}{\ln a} + C \\
&\int \sin x dx = -\cos x + C,\quad
\int \cos x dx = \sin x + C \\
&\int \sec^2 x dx = \tan x + C,\quad
\int \csc^2 x dx = -\cot x + C \\
&\int \frac{1}{x^2+a^2}dx = \frac{1}{a}\arctan\frac{x}{a} + C,\quad
\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}}dx = \arcsin\frac{x}{a} + C
\end{aligned}
$$

**分部积分法**
$$
\int u dv = uv - \int v du
$$

**换元积分法**
$$
\int f(\varphi(t))\varphi'(t)dt = \int f(x)dx \quad (x = \varphi(t))
$$

## 5. 级数
### 数项级数收敛
$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n = \lim_{n \to \infty} S_n,\ S_n = \sum_{k=1}^n a_k
$$

### 幂级数
$$
\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a_n(x-x_0)^n
$$

### 傅里叶级数
$$
f(x) \sim \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left(a_n\cos nx + b_n\sin nx\right)
$$
$$
a_n = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)\cos nxdx,\ b_n = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)\sin nxdx
$$

### 常用级数展开
**几何级数**
$$
\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} ar^n = \frac{a}{1-r} \quad (|r|<1)
$$

**指数级数**
$$
e^x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{n!} \quad (\forall x \in \mathbb{R})
$$

**三角函数级数**
$$
\begin{aligned}
&\sin x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (-1)^n \frac{x^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)!} \quad (\forall x \in \mathbb{R}) \\
&\cos x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (-1)^n \frac{x^{2n}}{(2n)!} \quad (\forall x \in \mathbb{R})
\end{aligned}
$$

**对数级数**
$$
\ln(1+x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (-1)^{n-1}\frac{x^n}{n} \quad (|x|<1)
$$

**二项式级数**
$$
(1+x)^\alpha = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \binom{\alpha}{n} x^n \quad (|x|<1,\ \alpha \in \mathbb{R})
$$

## 6. 多元微积分
### 偏导数
$$
\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_i} = \lim_{\Delta x_i \to 0} \frac{f(x_1,...,x_i+\Delta x_i,...,x_n)-f(x)}{\Delta x_i}
$$

### 全微分
$$
df = \sum_{i=1}^n \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_i}dx_i
$$

### 重积分
$$
\iint_D f(x,y)dxdy,\quad \iiint_{\Omega} f(x,y,z)dxdydz
$$

### 曲线/曲面积分
$$
\int_L f(x,y)ds,\quad \iint_{\Sigma} f(x,y,z)dS
$$

### 格林公式
$$
\oint_L Pdx+Qdy = \iint_D \left(\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}\right)dxdy
$$

### 高斯公式
$$
\oiint_{\Sigma} Pdydz+Qdzdx+Rdxdy = \iiint_{\Omega} \left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial Q}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial R}{\partial z}\right)dV
$$

### 斯托克斯公式
$$
\oint_{\Gamma} Pdx+Qdy+Rdz = \iint_{\Sigma} \left(\frac{\partial R}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial z}\right)dydz + \left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial z}-\frac{\partial R}{\partial x}\right)dzdx + \left(\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}\right)dxdy
$$

### 场论公式
**梯度**
$$
\nabla f = \left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial f}{\partial z}\right)
$$

**散度**
$$
\nabla \cdot \vec{F} = \frac{\partial F_x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial F_y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial F_z}{\partial z}
$$

**旋度**
$$
\nabla \times \vec{F} = \begin{vmatrix}
\vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\
F_x & F_y & F_z
\end{vmatrix}
$$

**拉普拉斯算子**
$$
\Delta f = \nabla^2 f = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z^2}
$$
